What is Pàtisserie?

Pâtisserie is used to describe
French pastries and the pastry
shop they are sold in. Although
the word is used quite liberally in
English-speaking countries, in
France and Belgium the law
restricts its use to bakeries who
employ licensed maître pâtissier
(master pastry chefs).

Specialty Coffee

We proudly serve Caffe Vita’s delicious
coffee blends and single origins. They are
pioneers in the Farm Direct movement
where coffees are sourced directly from
farmers that are committed to sustainable
practices. All coffees are roasted to order
and delivered weekly by our Brooklyn
based roaster.
